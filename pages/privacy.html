Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Janta Prints collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email, phone number, company name, and artwork files when you place an order or request a quote. We also collect standard web analytics data (page views, browser type) via Google Analytics.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to fulfil your orders, respond to enquiries, and send transactional notifications. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

Artwork Files

Files you upload for print orders are stored securely and used only for production purposes. Temporary files (Creative Studio) are deleted automatically within 24 hours. You may request deletion of your account and all associated files at any time.
