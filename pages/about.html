Our Story

Built in
Kisumu.
Built to Last.

Janta Prints was founded with a simple idea: Western Kenya deserves world-class print and branding services, delivered by people who know and love this region.

Mission

Why We
Do This.

Too many Kenyan businesses are sending their print orders to Nairobi — paying more, waiting longer, and dealing with vendors who don't understand local context. Janta Prints exists to change that.

We believe every business in Kisumu, every football club in Western Kenya, every event organiser on Lake Victoria's shores deserves access to print quality that rivals anything in the country — without leaving the region to get it.

Our Values.

Quality First

We don't ship anything we wouldn't be proud to hang on our own walls. Every order goes through a quality check before it leaves our studio.
