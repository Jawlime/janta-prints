Orders & Turnaround

Most standard products: 2–5 working days. Banners and roll-ups: same day or next day if submitted before 10am. Jerseys: 5–10 working days. Rush fees may apply for same-day requests.

Payment

We accept M-Pesa (preferred), bank transfer, and cash at our Kisumu studio. Corporate accounts can apply for credit terms. Payment is required before production begins for new clients.
